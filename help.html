Getting Started

Welcome to Promptly! Here's how to get started:

  1. Sign up for a free account
  2. Enter your niche or target audience
  3. Click "Generate Calendar" to create your content plan
  4. Customize and publish your content

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

What's included in the PRO plan?

Promptly Pro unlocks unlimited calendars, Brand Brain ingestion, caption/hashtag variants, regen-by-day controls, platform-specific scripts, downloadable asset folders, and priority support.

Can I export my content?

Yes! You can export as CSV, ICS calendar files, or download as a ZIP archive.
